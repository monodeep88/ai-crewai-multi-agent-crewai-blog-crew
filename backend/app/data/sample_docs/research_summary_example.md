### Research Summary for 'The Impact of AI on Modern Education'

**Key Findings:**
-   **Personalized Learning:** AI-powered platforms can adapt to individual student paces and learning styles, offering customized content and feedback. Examples: Khan Academy, Duolingo.
-   **Automated Grading & Feedback:** AI tools can grade assignments (especially multiple-choice, short answer) and provide instant feedback, freeing up educators' time. Challenges with essay grading remain.
-   **Administrative Efficiency:** AI can assist with scheduling, resource allocation, and student performance prediction, optimizing school operations.
-   **Access & Equity:** AI can break down barriers by providing educational resources to remote areas or students with special needs, though digital divide issues persist.
-   **Ethical Concerns:** Data privacy, algorithmic bias, job displacement for educators, and over-reliance on technology are significant concerns.
-   **Future Trends:** AI tutors, VR/AR enhanced learning, and predictive analytics for student success.

**Potential Angles:**
1.  AI as an 'assistant' to teachers, not a replacement.
2.  The dual challenge of leveraging AI while addressing ethical implications.
3.  Preparing students for an AI-driven future through AI education itself.
